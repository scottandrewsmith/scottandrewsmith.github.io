About Me

I was born and raised in Baltimore, Maryland and studied mathematics at the University of Maryland, completing my Phd in 2016 under the supervision of Konstantina Trivisa and Sandra Cerrai.

I was a postdoc in Leipzig, Germany from 2016-2019 at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences in the group of Felix Otto. I was a Van Vleck Visiting Assistant Professor from 2019 to 2021 at University of Wisconsin-Madison in the probability group, collaborating with Hao Shen.

Currently I'm an associate professor (tenure-track) at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Sciences within the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing.

Research

My work involves a mixture of analysis and probability, more specifically stochastic partial differential equations.

  • A new derivation of the finite N master loop equation for lattice Yang-Mills. (with Hao Shen, Rongchan Zhu), submitted (2022)
  • Phase transitions, logarithmic Sobolev inequalities, and uniform-in-time propagation of chaos for weakly interacting diffusions. (with Matias Delgadino, Rishabh S. Gvalani, Grigorios A. Pavliotis), submitted (2021)
  • A priori bounds for quasi-linear SPDEs in the full sub-critical regime. (with Felix Otto, Jonas Sauer, Hendrik Weber), submitted 2021
  • Large N limit of the O(N) linear sigma model via stochastic quantization. (with Hao Shen, Rongchan Zhu, Xiangchan Zhu) Annals of Probability (2022).
  • Parabolic equations with rough coefficients and singular forcing. (with Felix Otto, Jonas Sauer, and Hendrik Weber) (2018)
  • Stochastic continuity equations with conservative noise. (with Benjamin Gess) Journal de Mathematiques Pures et Appliquees (2019)
  • On the Boltzmann equation with stochastic kinetic transport: global existence of renormalized martingale solutions. (with Sam Punshon-Smith) Archive for Rational Mechanics and Analysis (2019)
  • Holder estimates for fractional parabolic equations with critical divergence free drifts. (with Matias Delgadino) Annales de l'Institute Henri Poincare C, Analyse non lineaire (2018)
  • The stochastic Navier-Stokes equations for heat-conducting, compressible fluids: global existence of weak solutions. (with Konstantina Trivisa) Journal of Evolution Equations (2018)
  • Random perturbations of viscous, compressible fluids: global existence of weak solutions. SIAM Journal of Mathematical Analysis (2017)
